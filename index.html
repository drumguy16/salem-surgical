Areas of Practice

Hernia

Hernias are enlarged natural openings in the body or separations of muscle, that allow underlying tissue to protrude through them.

Skin Cancer

Melanoma is a cancer of the pigment producing cells of the skin called melanocytes. The incidence of melanoma is increasing faster than any other malignancy.

Varicose Veins

The most common causes of varicose veins are pregnancy, pelvic or leg injuries, hip or knee surgery, heredity, and occupations where you stand or sit for prolonged periods of time.

Breast Lumps

Breast and lung cancer are the most common causes of cancer deaths in American women. About 12% of women will develop breast cancer during their lifetime.

Gallbladder Disease

Gallbladder symptoms range from mild, upper abdominal discomfort, indigestion, and nausea after eating, to severe upper abdominal pain that will penetrate into the back near the right shoulder blade.

Colon Cancer

Colon cancer strikes male and females equally. Around the world it is more frequent in industrialized countries, and this suggests genetic and environmental influences.

About Us

Salem Surgical is a general surgery group practice dedicated to providing our patients with the highest quality surgical care available.

Our doctors and professional staff at Salem Surgical strive for a "patient first" approach by designing a care plan that suits the patients needs and desires. The patient's safety and care come first. Our physicians and staff attend yearly conferences to stay abreast of the latest research and information on venous disease. Our facilities include an accredited vascular lab which allows patient's testing, diagnosis, and treatment to be done comfortably and conveniently, on site.
