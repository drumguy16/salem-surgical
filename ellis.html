Dr. Madge Ellis

M.D., F.A.C.S.

Dr. Madge Ellis is a board certified general surgeon from Jamaica. Dr. Ellis graduated from the British Commonwealth School of Medicine at the University of London in 1989, and then completed her general surgery training at The Pinnacle Health System in Harrisburg, Pa.  Dr. Ellis has advanced training in laparoscopic or minimal surgery, as well as the use of The Da Vinci Robot. Her interests include surgery of the stomach, intestines, spleen, and surgical treatment of Crohn’s Disease and Ulcerative Colitis.
