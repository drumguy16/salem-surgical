Dr. Robert Williams

M.D., F.A.C.S.

Dr. Williams is a board certified general surgeon who is the son of a navy pilot. Williams graduated from the Bowman Gray School of Medicine in 1987 and  completed his surgical internship and residency at the University of South Carolina in 1993. He has practiced in the Roanoke-Salem area since 1993 and co-founded Salem Surgical.  Dr. Williams is an expert in the diagnosis and treatment of breast cancer, and as such has been involved in research in the fight against breast cancer.
